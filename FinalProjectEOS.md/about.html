About me

Hello! My name is Nicole Daniel. I grew up in Idaho, spending a large
portion of my life in Boise. I first became interested in how things work at
an early age, eventually pursuing classes like Medical terminology and a
CNA class in order to become a certified nursing assistant. When I realized
that the medical path wasn't for me, I gained an interest in coding instead
because I wanted to know the ins and outs of how a computer works!
Today, I am currently enrolled in the Software Development program at
CWI and hope to use my degree to become a Web Developer in the future.
